About Me

I am a software developer with a creative mindset of an artist and an event planner. By combining my experience with Ruby, Javascript, Rails, and React.js/Flux, I am able to create innovative and sleek web applications.

I love to delve into the world of art. I am able to utilize my imagination to create innovative drawings while keeping an open mind and taking inspiration from others. Drawing is a continual learning experience for me--I actively pinpoint every mistake I make and constantly strive to improve. These qualities are extended to any project that I work one, whether it be teaching, event planning, or coding.
